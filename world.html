The World

The world is inspired by Jews and Jewish cultures around the world. But even if you are familiar with the myths, worry not! In this world, there is always something new to explore...

Loweland

In the warm planes of Loweland, people have gotten used to saftey and freedom. While it is not the MOST technologically advanced nation, it takes the second place for the innovation of their Judge Michael.

Betara

The nation of Betara is peaceful and prosperous. The Judge provides the nation with his guidence, and the sacred mountain provides the nation's life force. However, recently some areas of the nation begun to "wither"...

Orhanel

The two civilisations of Orhanel are finally united and at peace, and there are friendships that formed regardless of which side of the river you come from. But when the nation's most prized possession is stolen, old conflicts surface once again...

Zydovia

Always winter, always wonderland? The people of Zydovia were cut off from the rest of the world for decades, leading to poverty and struggle. But after a new Judge is appointed, could this status quo change?

Elle

Through blood, sweat and tears, people from all over the world managed to come to Elle, return to the basics, and blossom from there. Elle City is the crown jewel of this world, leading in almost every field, but of course, there are troubles in paradise. People cannot agree about the future for the city, and the conflicts become more and more violent. Can the Judge navigate in these treacherous waters?
