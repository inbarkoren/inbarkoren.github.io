The Characters

Click on the images to expand them.

Sarah - The Judge's Apprentice

The loyal apprentice and adopted daughter of Michael. With a constant smile in her face, she is always happy to help anyone in trouble.

Michael - Judge of Loweland

Michael always loved to experiment. Inspired by the technology from Elle City, he developed a new technology - golems. In his spare time, he tries to improve his technology, and sometimes he makes himself little golem buddies.

Malka - Judge of Orhanel

The people of Orhanel are known for their hospitality, and Malka is no exception. Even during the recovering of the nation from a civil war, Malka will always welcome guests into her home. Do try her soup! it took her years to perfect.

Natasha - Judge of Zydovia

Recently appointed as a Judge (not by choice, mind you), Natasha is, contrary to popular belief, trying her best. It's hard to be a Judge, and even harder to finally open the city back to the world after years of isolation.

Avraham - Judge of Betara

Avraham is known to all as the wisest and most reliable person in Betara. However, with age, he decided to stick just with "wisest", and be more of a guide to his nation. In his spare time, he enjoys playing with his pet leopard, that stood by his side for years.

Sarit - Judge of Elle

Sarit has... a lot of work to do. There's a whole city she needs to run, after all. However, she's always happy to finally kick back, relax, and enjoy a good smoke.
